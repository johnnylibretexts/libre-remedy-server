# DOCX Reading Order Coherence Judge v1

Evaluate whether the DOCX exposes a coherent linear reading sequence for assistive technology.

DOCX is mostly linear, so this dimension is a partial signal in v1. Focus on anomalies such as floating text boxes, positioned images, sidebars, captions, or tables that may be announced away from their surrounding context.

Return structured criterion scores only. Do not rate overall accessibility.
